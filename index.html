Restaurants

Osteria Francescana

Location: Via Stella, 22, Modena, Italy

Osteria Francescana is a world-renowned restaurant that blends traditional Italian cuisine with avant-garde culinary techniques. Chef Massimo Bottura offers a tasting menu that includes dishes like tortellini in cream of Parmigiano-Reggiano, five ages of Parmigiano-Reggiano, and reinterpretations of classic Italian flavors with a contemporary twist.

Gaggan

Location: 68/1 Soi Langsuan, Ploenchit Road, Bangkok, Thailand

Gaggan is a progressive Indian restaurant that has gained international acclaim for its boundary-pushing take on Indian cuisine. Chef Gaggan Anand's tasting menu is a playful exploration of Indian flavors, presented in a modern and unexpected way, with dishes like yogurt explosions, charcoal prawns, and innovative interpretations of street food
