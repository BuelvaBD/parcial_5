Restaurants

Noma

Refshalevej 96, Copenhagen, Denmark

Noma is a globally acclaimed restaurant known for its innovative approach to New Nordic cuisine. The menu is seasonal and revolves around foraging, fermentation, and local ingredients, offering dishes such as reindeer moss, sea buckthorn, and wild game. Noma is renowned for its experimental dishes and emphasis on the natural landscapes of Scandinavia.

Gaggan

Location: 68/1 Soi Langsuan, Ploenchit Road, Bangkok, Thailand

Gaggan is a progressive Indian restaurant that has gained international acclaim for its boundary-pushing take on Indian cuisine. Chef Gaggan Anand's tasting menu is a playful exploration of Indian flavors, presented in a modern and unexpected way, with dishes like yogurt explosions, charcoal prawns, and innovative interpretations of street food

Narisawa

Location: 2 Chome-6-15 Minamiaoyama, Minato City, Tokyo, Japan

Narisawa offers a unique dining experience that merges Japanese culinary traditions with French influences. The menu is centered around the concept of "innovative Satoyama cuisine," which emphasizes sustainability and harmony with nature. Dishes include seasonal ingredients prepared with meticulous care, such as forest-flavored broth and charcoal-grilled meat.

Central

Location: Av. Pedro de Osma 301, Barranco, Lima, Peru

Central is a restaurant that explores the diverse ecosystems of Peru through its innovative tasting menu. Chef Virgilio Martínez presents dishes that reflect the altitude and biodiversity of the country, with ingredients sourced from the Amazon, Andes, and coastal regions. The menu is an exploration of Peruvian culture and natural beauty, with dishes like sea urchin with cacao and Andean potatoes.

Attica

Location: 74 Glen Eira Road, Ripponlea, Melbourne, Australia

Attica is a modern Australian restaurant that emphasizes native ingredients and creative culinary techniques. Chef Ben Shewry's menu is an exploration of Australia's unique flora and fauna, with dishes like emu liver parfait, native honeycomb, and kangaroo with native herbs. The dining experience at Attica is both adventurous and deeply rooted in the natural environment.
